Help

Digital Banking FAQs

How do I register for *288#?+
Simply dial *288# from the phone number registered with your Ridgeways Finance account. You will be prompted to set a 4-digit PIN on first use. No prior registration is needed.
How do I download the mobile app?+
Search "Ridgeways Finance" on Google Play Store (Android) or Apple App Store (iOS). Download and install the free app, then log in with your member number and PIN.
What is the daily transaction limit?+
USSD: KSh 150,000 per day. Mobile App: KSh 300,000 per day. Web Portal: KSh 500,000 per day. Higher limits are available on request for verified members.
I forgot my PIN. What do I do?+
Dial *288# and select "Forgot PIN" or visit any branch with your National ID to reset your PIN. For security, PIN resets are not done over the phone.
Is USSD banking free?+
Standard network USSD charges may apply from your mobile operator (typically KSh 1–2 per session). Ridgeways Finance does not charge for USSD banking sessions.
What if I send money to the wrong account?+
Contact our customer care immediately on 0700 123 456. If the recipient has not withdrawn, we can reverse the transaction. We cannot guarantee reversal once funds are withdrawn.
