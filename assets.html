Requirements

Property / Land Finance+
Valid Title Deed (or Sale Agreement) · Valuation report from a registered valuer · National ID + KRA PIN · 3 months payslips or bank statements · Completed loan application form
Motor Vehicle Finance+
Logbook (original) · NTSA vehicle inspection report · Comprehensive insurance quote · National ID + KRA PIN · 3 months bank statements or payslips
Business Equipment Finance+
Proforma invoice from supplier · Business registration certificate · KRA PIN + National ID · 6 months business bank statements · Description of the equipment and its intended business use

Process

How Asset Finance Works

1

Identify Your Asset

Find the property, vehicle, or equipment you want to purchase and get a valuation or proforma invoice.

2

Submit Application

Complete the online or branch application form and attach all required documents.

3

Credit Assessment

Our credit team reviews your application and conducts due diligence on the asset within 3–5 working days.

4

Approval & Offer Letter

Upon approval, you receive a formal offer letter detailing the loan terms, rates, and conditions.

5

Disbursement

Funds are disbursed directly to the seller or supplier. You take ownership of the asset immediately.
