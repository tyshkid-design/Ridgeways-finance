Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 January 2025. This policy explains how Ridgeways Finance collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Ridgeways Finance is committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019 and all applicable regulations.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that is necessary to provide our financial services. This includes:

👤
Identity Information

Full name, date of birth, national ID or passport number, KRA PIN, and passport-sized photographs.

📞
Contact Information

Physical address, telephone number, email address, and next-of-kin contact details.

💰
Financial Information

Income details, bank statements, credit history, loan applications, and transaction records.

📱
Digital Usage Data

IP address, device information, login times, and activity logs when you use our app or website.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used exclusively for legitimate Sacco operations:

  • Processing membership applications and maintaining member records
  • Evaluating and processing loan applications
  • Managing your accounts and processing transactions
  • Sending account alerts, statements, and important notices
  • Complying with legal and regulatory obligations (KYC, AML)
  • Improving our products, services, and digital platforms
  • Communicating about new products, events, and updates (with your consent)
  • Fraud prevention and security monitoring

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your data with:

Regulatory & Government Authorities+
We are legally required to share information with SASRA, KRA, the Financial Reporting Centre (FRC), and law enforcement agencies where required by law, including for anti-money laundering (AML) and counter-terrorism financing (CTF) compliance.
Credit Reference Bureaus+
Loan performance data may be submitted to licensed Credit Reference Bureaus (CRBs) in Kenya as required by the Banking Act and CBK guidelines. This helps maintain credit market integrity.
Technology & Service Providers+
We work with vetted technology partners for mobile banking, SMS alerts, and payment processing. These partners are contractually bound to handle your data securely and only for the purposes we specify.
Auditors & Legal Advisors+
Our external auditors and legal advisors may access member data as required to perform their professional duties. All such parties are bound by strict confidentiality obligations.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data:

🔐

256-bit SSL Encryption

All data transmission is encrypted using bank-grade protocols.

🛡️

Access Controls

Role-based access ensures staff only see data necessary for their role.

🔍

Audit Trails

All system access and data changes are logged and monitored.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your membership is active and for the periods required by law thereafter:

Data TypeRetention Period
Member registration recordsDuration of membership + 7 years
Loan application records10 years after loan closure
Transaction records7 years (statutory requirement)
Digital access logs2 years
CCTV footage (branches)90 days

6. Your Rights

Under the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019, you have the following rights:

👁️
Right of Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you at any time.

✏️
Right to Rectification

Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

🗑️
Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your data, subject to legal and regulatory retention requirements.

🚫
Right to Object

Object to processing of your data for marketing purposes at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@ridgewaysfinance.co.ke or write to us at our head office.

7. Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies to ensure proper functionality (e.g., session management, security tokens). We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. You may disable non-essential cookies through your browser settings without affecting your ability to use our services.

8. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will notify members via email and a notice on our website at least 30 days before the changes take effect. Continued use of our services after that date constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
