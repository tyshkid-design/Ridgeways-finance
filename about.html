Who We Are

A Member-Owned Financial Institution

Ridgeways Finance Sacco Society Limited is a deposit-taking Sacco licensed and regulated by the Sacco Societies Regulatory Authority (SASRA). We were established in Ridgeways, Nairobi in 2008 by a group of passionate community members who believed that affordable finance should be accessible to everyone — not just the few.

Today, with over 12,000 active members and a growing asset base exceeding KSh 3.2 billion, we remain true to our founding principle: every member is an owner, and every owner shares in our prosperity through annual dividends and rebates.

Our Journey

From Humble Beginnings
to a Thriving Institution

2008

Founding

Ridgeways Finance was founded by 47 pioneering members in Ridgeways, Nairobi with a vision of community-led financial empowerment.

2010

First Branch Expansion

Opened our second branch to serve the growing membership base in adjacent Nairobi neighbourhoods.

2012

SASRA Licensing

Received our deposit-taking Sacco licence from SASRA, becoming one of the regulated Saccos in Kenya.

2015

Asset Base Hits KSh 500M

Surpassed KSh 500 million in total assets and launched our first digital banking product via USSD.

2018

Mobile App Launch

Launched the Ridgeways Finance mobile app on Android and iOS, enabling 24/7 banking for our members.

2020

COVID-19 Response

Introduced emergency loan moratoriums and digital-only account opening to support members during the pandemic.

2022

KSh 2 Billion Milestone

Crossed KSh 2 billion in total assets and launched the Diaspora Account product for members abroad.

2025

KSh 3.2 Billion & 12K Members

Today we serve over 12,000 active members with a full suite of digital and branch-based financial products.

Regulation & Governance

Fully Licensed & Regulated

Your money is in safe hands. Ridgeways Finance is fully licensed by SASRA under the Sacco Societies Act and adheres to all prudential guidelines governing deposit-taking Saccos in Kenya.
