FAQs

Loan Questions Answered

How soon can I get my first loan?+
You can apply for a loan after 3 months of active membership and having made consistent monthly contributions. Supersaver loans are available after just 1 month.
What collateral do I need?+
Your savings balance serves as primary collateral. For larger amounts, you will need one or more guarantors who are Ridgeways Finance members in good standing.
Can I repay my loan early?+
Yes. There is no early repayment penalty. You will only pay interest for the months the loan was outstanding, saving you money on the remaining balance.
What happens if I miss a repayment?+
A penalty of 5% is charged on the outstanding instalment. We encourage members to contact us before missing a payment — we can help restructure your repayment schedule.
Can I have more than one loan?+
Yes, subject to your repayment capacity and total outstanding balance not exceeding the approved multiplier of your shares and savings.
