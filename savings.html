Tips

Smart Saving Habits

📅

Pay Yourself First

Set up an automatic monthly contribution on payday before spending on anything else. Even KSh 1,000 a month builds up over time.

🎯

Save With a Goal

Members with a named savings goal save 3× more than those without one. Use our Akiba account to tie your savings to a specific purpose.

📈

Let Interest Compound

Avoid premature withdrawals. Letting interest accumulate in your fixed deposit or savings account unlocks the power of compounding over time.
