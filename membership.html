Become a Member

Join over 12,000 members who trust Ridgeways Finance with their financial futures. Membership is open to all Kenyan citizens — residents and Diaspora.

✅ Open to All Kenyans 📱 Apply Online ⚡ Fast Processing
Why Join?

Membership Benefits

As a member-owned institution, every benefit we offer goes directly to you.

💰

Annual Dividends

Share in our profits. Members earn dividends on shares held at the end of each financial year.

🏦

Loan Access

Access development, business, salary, and asset finance loans at rates from 1% p.m.

📈

High Interest Savings

Earn up to 8.5% P.A. on fixed deposits and competitive rates on all savings accounts.

📱

Digital Banking

Free access to our mobile app, USSD *288#, and web portal — bank anywhere, anytime.

🛡️

FOSA Cover

Life insurance cover on loans and savings for qualifying members and their dependants.

🤝

Community

Be part of a thriving financial community. Vote at AGMs and shape the Sacco's direction.

📚

Financial Education

Free access to our financial literacy workshops, webinars, and advisory services.

🎁

Member Welfare

Access to our benevolent fund for support during hospitalisation, bereavement, and emergencies.

How to Join

3 Simple Steps

1

Fill the Application Form

Complete the online membership form below, or download a paper form from any of our branches. Attach the required documents.

2

Pay the Registration Fee

Pay the one-time registration fee of KSh 1,000 and a minimum share capital of KSh 5,000 via M-Pesa Paybill 700288 or at the branch.

3

Get Your Member Number

Your application is processed within 2–3 working days. You'll receive your member number via SMS and can immediately activate digital banking.

📋
Documents Required: Copy of National ID / Passport · KRA PIN Certificate · Passport photo · Proof of address (utility bill or bank statement)

Online Membership Application

Fill in your details and we'll contact you within 24 hours.
